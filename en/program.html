Fight Night

A very important part of the Dutch juggling convention is ofcourse the fight night! Friday night, jugglers with all sorts of backgrounds will come together to 'fight' each other. The rules are simple: firstly, both players will start juggling. Then, throughout the game, both jugglers will try to make sure the other drops their clubs using various techniques. The person who is juggling last, wins! This exciting game is a unique experience for the audience as well that you do not want to miss out on!

Games

On Saturday afternoon, at the Markt in Wijchen, the games are held. These include a variety of playful or very serious competitions, such as high jumping on a unicycle, who can juggle 5 balls the longest, who can stand on their hands the longest, or who can juggle 3 coins the longest. These competitions are open to all levels, making it fun for passers-by to watch or even participate!

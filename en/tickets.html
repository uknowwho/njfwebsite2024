Tickets

The tickets for NJF 2024 are available during various phases. The first phase runs from January 22nd to February 25th. The second phase runs from February 26th to April 21st. After April 21st, pre-registration is still possible but only at the door price. All Saturday tickets include a Gala Show ticket worth 15 euros. With all tickets, except for single-day tickets, breakfast and dinner are included. Please note that there is no breakfast on Thursday and no dinner on Sunday due to the start and end times of the NJF.

Het Supportticket

This year, NJF introduces the Support Ticket. This is a ticket for the entire festival that, at a higher rate, ensures that NJF remains accessible and affordable for everyone. Thanks to those who purchase this ticket, the organization can create an even more beautiful, cooler, and more enjoyable festival. Support Tickets are available during the first phase starting from 130,-. During the second phase, the initial Support Ticket costs 135,-. Benefactors at the door can obtain a Support Ticket starting from 145,-.
